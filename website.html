ABOUT: How is math important to the world?


WORLD: Math is important because it is the most widely used subject in the world. Every career uses some sort of math. More importantly, doing math helps the mind to reason and organized complicated situations or problems into clear, simple, and logical steps.

ALGEBRA 1

The two main topics of Algebra 1 are polynomial factoring and inequalities. Some potential jobs for Algebra 1 are architecture, civil engineering, business, and teaching.

TRIGONOMETRY


The three main topics of Trigonometry are statistics, logarithms, and quadratics. Some potential jobs for Trigonometry are navigators, radiologist, surveyors, and teaching.


GEOMETRY

The three main topics of Geometry are right triangles, segments in triangles, and transformation.Some potential jobs for Geometry are transportation industry, construction industry, art, architecture, and medicine.
